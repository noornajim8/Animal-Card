Cat

Cats can see in light levels six times lower than what is required for human vision, thanks to a special reflective layer behind their retinas called the tapetum lucidum.

  • Scientific Name: Felis catus
  • Average Length: 18 to 20 inches
  • Average Lifespan: 12 to 15 years
  • Habitat: human-dominated environments, including homes, farms, and cities
